Our Story

Founded in 2025, Greens & Protein started as a passion project in Mumbai, India. Tired of boring, unhealthy food delivery options, Ashish and Tushar created a brand focused on affordable, protein-rich vegetarian meals. What began as home-kitchen experiments turned into a full-fledged healthy food service within 6 months.

Why Protein Bowls?

Each bowl is carefully crafted with high-protein ingredients like sprouts, paneer, seeds, and curd. These bowls help:

  • Build muscle and aid recovery
  • Keep you full longer (control cravings)
  • Support fat loss and metabolism

Weight Loss Through Salads

Low in calories and high in nutrients, protein salads are ideal for those looking to lose weight without starving. Combined with portion control and regular activity, these bowls can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Macronutrients Simplified

Proteins: Help build tissues and muscle. Vital for growth and repair.

Carbohydrates: Body's main source of energy. Found in vegetables, grains, and legumes.

Fats: Necessary for hormones and brain health. Choose healthy fats from seeds, nuts, and dairy.
